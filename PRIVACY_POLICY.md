# Privacy Policy for LuminaVerse

**Last Updated:** January 5, 2026

## Overview

LuminaVerse is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our mobile application.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

LuminaVerse:

- Does NOT collect personal information
- Does NOT track user behavior
- Does NOT use analytics services
- Does NOT share data with third parties
- Does NOT require user accounts or login

## Local Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

- Favorite/bookmarked verses
- Reading streak information
- Notification preferences (time and enabled status)
- Music settings

This data never leaves your device and is stored using your device's secure storage system.

## Third-Party Services

LuminaVerse uses the following third-party services to function:

- **bible-api.com**: Provides Bible verse content
- **labs.bible.org**: Fallback Bible verse provider
- **Google Actions Audio Library**: Background music streaming (optional feature)

These services may have their own privacy policies. We do not send any personal information to these services - only requests for Bible verses or audio content.

## Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:

- **Notifications**: To send daily verse reminders (optional, user-controlled)
- **Internet Access**: To fetch Bible verses and stream background music

## Children's Privacy

LuminaVerse does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for all ages.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted in this document and within the app.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

- Email: support@labazine.com

## Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no data to access, modify, or delete. All app data is stored locally on your device and can be removed by uninstalling the app.

---

**Summary**: LuminaVerse is a privacy-first app. We don't collect, store, or share any of your personal information. All your data stays on your device.
