## App Store Review Notes

**For Apple App Store Reviewers:**

Thank you for reviewing LuminaVerse.

**Purpose:**
LuminaVerse is a minimalist Bible verse application designed for daily spiritual inspiration and meditation. The app intentionally focuses on simplicity and ease-of-use to provide a distraction-free devotional experience.

**Key Features to Test:**

1. **Home Screen**: Tap refresh to load new random verses. Verses are fetched from bible-api.com (primary) with labs.bible.org as fallback.

2. **Favorites**: Tap the heart icon on any verse to save it. View saved verses in the Favorites tab. Swipe left on any favorite to delete or share.

3. **Reading Streaks**: Open the app on consecutive days to build your reading streak. The golden lightning badge shows your current streak, with a detailed card display in Settings.

4. **Notifications**: In Settings, enable daily reminders and choose your preferred time. _Note: Notifications work in production builds but not in Expo Go during development._

5. **Background Music**: Toggle ambient sounds in Settings. _Note: This feature works in production builds but may not function in development mode._

6. **Offline Support**: Once verses are loaded, they can be accessed without internet connection.

**Test Account:**
Not required - the app has no login or user accounts.

**API Information:**

- Bible verses: bible-api.com (public domain translations)
- Fallback: labs.bible.org
- Background audio: Google Actions Audio Library (optional feature)

**Privacy:**
No data collection or tracking. All user data (favorites, streaks, settings) stored locally on device using AsyncStorage.

**Differentiation:**
Unlike complex Bible apps with hundreds of features, LuminaVerse focuses on one thing: providing a moment of daily inspiration without distraction. Key differentiators:

- No account required
- No ads or subscriptions
- Reading streak gamification
- One-tap random verse discovery
- Peaceful music integration
- Minimalist, meditation-focused interface

**Content Rights:**
All Bible verses are from public domain translations. Attribution provided in Settings screen.

4+ (No objectionable content)
